Welcome to EvolutionaryOntology.org

What are you looking for?

This domain is intended for information on the research and development of ontologies and other vocabularies relevant to evolution. Currently there is only one ontology project (CDAO) hosted on this domain, but here are links to various projects:

  • CDAO, the Comparative Data Analysis Ontology
  • The concept glossary for evolutionary comparative analysis developed by the NESCent EvoInfo working group
  • The Evolution Ontology, a project of Adam Goldstein
  • The Homology Ontology (via Ontobee), a project from Marc Robison-Rechavi's lab
